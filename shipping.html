Policy

Shipping & Delivery.

Free across India. 3–7 working days. COD across 25,000+ pincodes.

Delivery estimates by region

  • Metro (Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune, Ahmedabad) — 3–5 working days
  • Tier 2 cities — 5–6 working days
  • Tier 3 / rural / Northeast / Andamans — 6–9 working days

Use the pincode checker on the home page for an exact ETA on your address.

Dispatch timeline

Orders placed before 6 PM IST on a working day are dispatched the same day. Orders after 6 PM, on Sundays, or on national holidays dispatch the next working day.

Couriers we use

  • Delhivery — primary for metro and Tier 2
  • Bluedart — for time-sensitive Tier 2 / Tier 3
  • Ekart — for Flipkart-network pincodes
  • India Post — for last-mile rural pincodes where private couriers don't reach

Tracking

You receive a tracking ID via SMS and WhatsApp within 2 hours of dispatch. You can also check live status on our order tracking page using your CHOP-XXXXXXXX order ID.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

  • Available across 25,000+ pincodes.
  • ₹50 handling fee added at checkout.
  • Pay the courier in cash or by UPI on the day of delivery.
  • For COD orders, our team confirms by WhatsApp before dispatch.

Address changes

You can change your delivery address only before dispatch. WhatsApp us within 6 hours of placing the order. After dispatch, addresses are locked with the courier.

Delivery attempts

Couriers attempt delivery up to 3 times. If all attempts fail, the package returns to us. We'll WhatsApp you to confirm a new address — re-shipping is free.

Damaged on arrival

If the outer box looks tampered or crushed, refuse the delivery and WhatsApp us a photo. We dispatch a replacement immediately at no cost.
