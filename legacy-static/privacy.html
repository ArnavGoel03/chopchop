Privacy Policy.

Effective 17 May 2026 · Last updated 17 May 2026

What we collect

When you place an order or use this site, we collect:

  • Identity & contact — name, phone number, delivery address, optional email and GSTIN.
  • Payment — handled by Razorpay (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified). We never see or store your card or UPI credentials.
  • Order data — what you bought, when, and any discount codes used.
  • Technical — IP address, browser, device type, referring page. Used for fraud prevention and analytics.
  • Marketing — via Meta Pixel, if you arrived from a Facebook or Instagram ad, so we can measure ad performance.

Why we collect it

  • To fulfill your order — courier handover, COD verification, GST invoice.
  • To send order updates via SMS and WhatsApp.
  • To process returns and warranty claims.
  • To comply with Indian tax law (records retained for 7 years).
  • To improve our products, ads, and site performance.

Who we share with

  • Couriers — Delhivery, Bluedart, Ekart, India Post for delivery only.
  • Payment processor — Razorpay for online payment processing.
  • SMS / WhatsApp providers — for order updates.
  • Tax authorities — as required by Indian law.
  • Meta / Google — anonymized event signals (page view, purchase) for ad optimization. You can opt out by disabling cookies.

We do not sell your data. Ever.

How we store it

Order records live on servers in India. Payment data lives only with Razorpay, never on our infrastructure. Backups are encrypted.

Your rights

  • Request a copy of all data we hold on you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion (subject to tax retention rules — we cannot delete invoices for 7 years).
  • Opt out of marketing SMS / WhatsApp at any time by replying STOP.

Cookies

We use cookies for session continuity, fraud prevention, and (if enabled) Meta Pixel ad measurement. You can clear cookies in your browser settings; the site will still work but ad attribution will be lost.
