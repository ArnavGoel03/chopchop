Policy

Returns & Refunds.

7-day no-questions returns. We arrange pickup.

The short version

Don't love it? WhatsApp us within 7 days of delivery. We arrange free pickup. Refund hits your account 48 hours after we receive it back.

How to start a return

  1. WhatsApp +91 99999 99999 with your order ID (e.g. CHOP-AB1234).
  2. Tell us why you're returning — one line is enough.
  3. We send a courier to your address within 48 hours.
  4. Pack the chopper in its original box if possible. If lost, any sturdy box works.

When you get your refund

  • UPI / Card / Net Banking: 48 hours after we receive the product. Refunded to the same source.
  • Wallet / EMI: 3–5 working days, depending on bank.
  • Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer or UPI to an account you confirm via WhatsApp. 48 hours after receipt.

What's not eligible

  • Product damaged during use (cracked body from drops, blades damaged by chopping ice or bones).
  • Missing parts (bowl, lid, blade assembly, base).
  • Returns initiated after 7 days from delivery.
  • Hygiene-sealed accessories opened and used.

Warranty claims (different from returns)

For manufacturing defects after the 7-day window, the 1-year warranty applies. Send a photo of the defect via WhatsApp. We replace the part or unit free of charge. See our Terms for full warranty scope.

Cancellations before dispatch

If your order hasn't shipped yet, WhatsApp us — we cancel and refund in full within 24 hours. After dispatch, treat it as a return.
